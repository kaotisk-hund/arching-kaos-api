Welcome to the API of Arching Kaos Radio

Upload a show with IPFS

You need to have your show uploaded to IPFS in OggVorbis format*. Also, save our swarm key at your IPFS configuration folder (usually ~/.ipfs).

In case you haven't uploaded before or you are not a registered uploader, visit #radio @ irc.ed12.ga IRC chat




* Having little space at our servers, we recommend 128-192kbps encoding for your OggVorbis** formatted file.
** If you wish your show to have available metadata while playing on the radio, you need to add the 'artist' and 'title' tags.

Recommended tool for converting to oggVorbis

Audacity
